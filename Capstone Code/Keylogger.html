Keyloggers

A keylogger is a monitor that monitors activity. It creates records of everything

you type on a keyboard or any device. It is used to silently monitor your activity

on the keyboard. It is important to be aware that keyloggers can be used with malicious

intent.

Keyloggers are used to help monitor your employees. This is especially beneficial when you

have a smaller business and cannot afford any legal disputes or reputational risks. Keyloggers give businesses full

transparency of what their employees will be doing on work computers, tablets or smartphones. Further, when the

users know they are being monitored it creates full disclosure and honesty amongst workers and management. With the

users knowing they are being monitored this has led to an increase in employee productivity. The monitoring employs a sense of

responsibility by discouraging downtime, social media websites or games. This will give a further understanding of performance.

By monitoring your employees, employers are able to understand what your staff is working on throughout the week, help find

and recognize anyone that is lacking and/or going above and beyond.

Another way keyloggers can be beneficial is by protecting your company from data theft by monitoring. Furthermore, with

the complications of constantly having to comply with best cybersecurity practices; keyloggers can be beneficial when employees

lose track of their passwords-- the admin can just look back at your keylogger data and recover forgotten passwords promptly.

Businesses are constantly at risk with an increase in phishing and virus attacks on networks. Therefore, it is easier

for companies to be able to look and monitor which members of the organization are clicking on risky emails or links.

There are a lot of ways in which keyloggers can be beneficial to any size company. However, it seems almost essential

for every small business to protect themselves by adding on a keylogger.
