CIA Triad

The CIA triad is a security model designed to help people develop

their understanding about various parts of IT security.

CIA - Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability

Confidentiality

Confidentiality involves secure information

that is only accessed by authorized users. It

is about controlling access to data in order to

prevent unauthorized disclosure and access.

Integrity

Integrity involves secure information

or systems that are always accurate.

It ensures that the information has not been

tampered with. The data has to be correct,

accurate, and reliable.

Availability

Availability involves secure information

that is always accessible to anybody who needs it.

This concept also means that networks, applications,

and systems are up and running.
