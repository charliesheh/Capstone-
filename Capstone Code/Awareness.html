Awareness Costs

We are aware that cybersecurity problems are becoming

an everyday struggle for companies, especially for small

businesses that can't afford a cybersecurity team. Proper

cybersecurity awareness training is very crucial to prevent

any kind of threat.

Costs

Typically, the price for cybersecurity training can range from free to

$5000 (or more). The price ultimately depends on the quality of training

and how much access to hands-on labs/exercises will be supplied. However,

more expensive training costs can most likely lead to more valuable credentials

like certificates or college degrees.


There are some cybersecurity training resources that are relatively inexpensive.

These ones include Cybrary and Udemy.


Cost of Keyloggers

There are several types of keyloggers and keylogging software out there:

Spyrix Free Keylogger
This software happens to be the free version of the paid Spyfix Personal Monitor.
Features include taking screenshots, clipboards, and capturing keystrokes.
Advanced features such as social network monitoring, log delivery, and
alerts are unavailable.

KidLogger
This software is completely free and an open-source user activity monitoring tool.
It can be utilized for logging keystrokes and clipboard. It can also record USB drives,
folder usage, and files. Another great feature is that you can record sound from a microphone
and take screenshots.
