So, what exactly is cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is a field in information technology that practices

protecting systems and senstive data from digital attacks, information

information disclosure, theft, breaches, etc.

Cybersecurity is vital, because organizations related to military, government,

and medical fields collect, process, and store new amounts of data on computers

and other devices. It is very likely that a large portion of that data is sensitive.

Examples of sensitive data include credit card information, intellectual property, etc.

Cybersecurity is a field that is dedicated to protecting that information and the

systems utilized to store it.
